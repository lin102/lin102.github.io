About me

Welcome! My name is Lin Che, a GIS professional and a Cartographer. Currently, I am a Senior R&D Engineer in the GPS Big Data Mining Group at DiDi. My research interests include Spatio-Temporal Data Mining and modeling, GIS Applications, and Web Mapping.

I earned my Master's degree in Cartography from Technical University of Munich (TUM), During my Master's program, I studied in three prestigious institutions in Europe - TUM, Vienna University of Technology (TUW), and Technical University of Dresden (TUD). Before coming to Europe, I completed my Bachelor at Wuhan University (WHU), China, majored in Geographic Information Science (GIS).
